Edit

Email Subject
^^^^^^^^^^^^^

The subject of the email.

(content)
^^^^^^^^^

The content of the Template.

Enable template?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

When set to enable, this template will be used, in place of an auto generated Email in the users language.
